Together Stream

—
Your ticket to watching Youtube videos together.

A Social Streaming Experience

Together Stream is an app that brings people together to watch videos in real time. Integrated with YouTube, the host has full freedom to choose, pause, and skip and video — syncing immediately with friends' devices.

Host a Stream

When you host a stream with Together Stream, you get to play DJ and change the mood.

Not interested in hosting? Join other Together Streamers and see what they're watching.

Media Sync Stream

—
Powered by IBM's Contextual Sync, Media Sync Stream is an app that allows users to watch video content together in real time.

Contextual sync is an IBM open source, real-time, continuous data sync service for web, IoT, and mobile.
